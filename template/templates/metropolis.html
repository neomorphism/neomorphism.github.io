Neumorphism

Neumorphism (or Neo-skeuomorphism) is a modern iteration of a style of designing web elements, frames, screens, etc. known as Skeuomorphism. SO, Neumorphism is a witty (right?) combo of the words “New” and “Skeuomorphism”. Since “New” isn’t a new word to us, let’s look at “Skeuomorphism”.
